Privacy Policy

Ali3N Network Scanner Pro

Last updated: December 2025

Developer: Jonathan Lockhart

Ali3N Network Scanner Pro (“the App”) is a local network analysis tool. It is designed to help you discover devices on your own network, scan ports, generate reports, and perform Wake-on-LAN operations.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is stored. The App does not collect or transmit personal data to the developer or to any third parties.

1. Information the App Does Not Collect

The App does not:

  • Collect or store personal data on external servers.
  • Send network scan results to the developer.
  • Use advertising SDKs, trackers, or analytics services.
  • Collect GPS location, contacts, SMS, or media.
  • Store any information beyond what is required locally for app functionality.

2. Information the App Does Access

a) Local Network Information

To perform network scanning, the App may access:

  • Your device’s local IP address and subnet information.
  • Devices discovered on your local network (e.g., IP, MAC, host name, vendor where available).
  • Open ports on discovered devices, when you choose to run port scans.

This information is used only to display results to you in the App and to create optional local CSV/PDF reports. All processing happens on your device and is not transmitted externally.

b) Location Permission (Technical Requirement)

On modern versions of Android, access to Wi-Fi SSID and certain network details requires location-related permissions (such as ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION or ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION).

Ali3N Network Scanner Pro requests these permissions solely so it can determine your local network parameters and perform scans. The App does not attempt to determine or track your physical location, nor does it send any location information to the developer or to third parties.

c) Saved Scans and Notes

If you choose to save scan results or add notes/tags to devices, this data is stored locally on your device. You may delete saved scans or clear app data at any time using your device’s operating system settings.

d) Wake-on-LAN (WOL)

When you use the Wake-on-LAN feature, the App sends a broadcast “magic packet” on your local network using the target device’s MAC address. No WOL-related data is sent outside your local network or to the developer.

3. Data Sharing

The App does not share data with:

  • The developer (no servers involved).
  • Advertising networks.
  • Analytics providers.
  • Social networks or other third parties.

Any CSV or PDF exports you generate are stored or shared only through apps or locations you select (for example, email, cloud storage, etc.). Once exported, handling of those files is governed by the policies of the apps or services you choose.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for technical users and network administrators. It is not designed for children under 16. As the App does not collect personal data, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

5. Security

Because the App does not send data to external servers, traditional server-side storage security is not applicable. Network scan results are processed in memory and optionally stored locally on your device as part of saved scans or exported files. You are responsible for securing your device and any exported files (e.g., CSV or PDF) that you choose to save or share.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in functionality or legal requirements. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly. Significant changes may also be mentioned in the App’s release notes on the Google Play Store.
